I wrote a post on Toxic Positivity<\/a> because that is what I find very prevalent in the spiritual world. A lot of rah-rah-We-so-happy-clappy<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n

But I found a bit of dissatisfaction with the post; though the spiritual world is sickeningly positive, the wider world is constantly and toxically negative:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Most social media is full of constant attacks, attempts to “cancel” the other side and spreading of fake news<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Most commentary\/reviews of books are harshly negative. I’ve stopped watching movie reviews on YouTube as they are full of useless nitpicking, trolling or attempting to start a “culture war” over a minor 10 second scene in a 2 hour movie<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • News, especially on TV channels, is full of constant The-sky-is-falling <\/em>scare mongering, or constant demonising the other political side. Much of it isn’t even news anymore but opinion masquerading as facts.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Most companies\/work places have a very doom-and-gloom outlook, a culture of blame and pushing problems under rugs.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Relationships, especially romantic, are full of blaming and expecting the worst.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    The last two things are particularly harmful. So many relationships fail due to a very negative and entitled opinions of both partners. And people blame their work environment for all their problems, not realising that by just complaining and moaning they are a part of the problem.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    (Related: It isn’t your job’s role to make you happy [LINK])<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The harm of this negativity to our society and health<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    The harms to our society are very clear in the social media age: People spend their time moaning and bitching online but don’t take any steps to fix the issues.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    And people living in first world countries in all the comforts now feel they are living in the worst of times, how there is no hope for humanity etc etc<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    And this is leading to mental problems like depression, low self-esteem, insomnia, substance abuse. And it is leading to new problems like the incel culture.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
