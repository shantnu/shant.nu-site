This is a big number as the course was open to everyone\u2014plumbers, doctors, engineers, and marketers. And yet, a large percentage of people wanted to make money on LoA.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It seems the people selling us \u201cmanifesting\u201d are the only ones \u201cmanifesting\u201d any wealth. It\u2019s like these sleazy online get rich courses, that teach you how to create and sell sleazy online get rich courses.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
